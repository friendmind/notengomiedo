Somethings About Me

These are somethings I believe you should know about me.

I'm sure it's a mix of so many things but I am not very good at fighting against the urge of pulling away and disappearing from people's lives. I am working on it but sometimes it comes through and makes an appearance.

Of course, I am careful with what I do and say as I am aware of the impact these things have. But there are times when my emotions take over and I simply do things that hurt others and/or myself. But unlike most people, I am fully aware of what I do and don't do.

So yes, I was aware of the intentions my words had when I sent those texts. They were misinterpreted, which I did not see coming. But that is besides the point. You were going through it and I projected my fears and worries onto you. So your reaction was valid.

How I mentioned above, I was isolated for a while. During this period, I distanced myself from people, in general, for my own good and for their own good. With time, things have begun to change for me. You know a bit regarding that. It is rare for me to let myself get close to anyone. But when it does occur, I end up having this profound intensity of cherishment for them that can be good and bad.
